What is your website about?

I want to help people make healthier choices, including myself. I will be posting weekly workouts, recipes and motivation for people. With the recipes containing calorie counts and nutritional breakdowns. This will be the ultimate guide to living your best healthy life.

What is the objective of your website?

There has been a recent uptick in the ammount of people focusing on fitness and getting themselves healthy since covid and lock-down happened. I have also shifted my lifestyle to be healthier. I want to make this website to not only motivate me but also help other people stay on the healthy wave. On the recipe page I am hoping to set up a random recipe generator, as I am a very indecisive person and never know what I want to eat, so this could be an added feature I could go back to use in the future.

Who is your target audience?

My target audience is other young girls either hoping to start their fitness journey or to help those struggling to stay on their journey.

  • Page 2 - Recipes (Sweet Treats)
  • Page 3 - Workouts
  • Page 4 - Skincare Tips
